About

Results-Driven. Efficient. Innovative.

I am a motivated and determined individual with 10 years of professional experience ranging from retail to pharmaceutical sales to computer technologies. I have used my team oriented attitude throughout my career to manage, coach, and train employees. I have enhanced sales for multiple (3) billion-dollar pharmaceutical drugs, as well as sustained sales volume after the drugs went generic.

Recently I have begun training at Eleven Fifty Academy in an accelerated learning program focusing on programming, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and C#. Included in this program is experience working in the Agile/Scrum development methodology. I have utilized and received training on Sublime Text, Git, and Visual Studio.

My colleagues recognize me as an industrious worker who likes to be highly efficient while delivering top-notch results. I consistently look to improve the status quo, including taking every opportunity to learn new skills. Self-motivated and proactive, I complete tasks with little-to-no supervision, but enjoy working with a talented to team to accomplish goals.

I have a B.S. from the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University in Finance/Real Estate.

I spend most of my free time with my two sons and wife. With a passion for sports, I enjoy watching football on Sunday (Go Colts!) and thoroughly love watching IU basketball. I enjoy watching shows such as The Walking Dead, Game of Thrones, and Archer. I am also a big fan of comics and the movies based on them. Other pastimes include golfing and video games.

I am currently learning as much as possible about the Information and Software industries. Whether you're a recruiter, potential employer, or looking for a new contact feel free to contact me through my information on this website.
